Our Story

Fit and Fab Gym was founded in 2002 as a family owned and operated business. The Fit and Fab Gym founders didn’t
want it to be just another gym - they wanted to be the best in the industry and set their minds to doing so! Since
its birth, Fit and Fab Gym has grown into one of Australia’s largest gym having helped over 50,000 customers live
longer, happier and healthier lives.



What We Do

We want to help you live a fit and healthy lifestyle! We do this by helping you find the most suitable exercises
and diet for your home gym, studio or commercial gym, keeping your budget, lifestyle and fitness goals in mind.



Customer Service

At Gym and Fitness, customers are at the centre of what we do and why we do it! We have a team of knowledgeable trainers
who are ready to assist you with your fitness needs. Our crew will be able to answer your questions from honest product
information to the best way to integrate a piece of equipment into your workout routine. We work hard to ensure we have
strong relationships with our customers, bringing you the best possible in the industry.
